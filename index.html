Teachers

NIS has a team of carefully chosen qualified and experienced teachers, who take great pleasure in guiding each student with personal attention and devotion. To add a truly international dimension, we have an established policy of inviting guest teachers from different countries to our school. We have had guest teachers from Poland, Czechoslovakia, USA, Greece, UK and Lithuania coming to us via AIESEC (Association internationale des étudiants en sciences économiques et commerciales) programme. Exchange interns help in providing a global perspective to our students by interacting with them and conducting various workshops on various issues.
